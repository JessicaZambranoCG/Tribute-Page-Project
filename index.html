Mr. Ruben Antonio Zambrano

"Father of the whole family, exemplary man."

The life of Mr. Ruben Zambrano is described here:

  • 1957 - He was born in the mines of Lobatera, Tachira state

  • 1969 - At the age of 12 he began to work planting and cultivating the land

  • 1974 - He travels to Caracas and gets married, his wife's name Ana Zambrano

  • 1977 - He has his first child, his son's name Jose Antonio Zambrano

  • 1983 - He has his second daughter, named Jessica Zambrano.

  • 2000 - Buy a land in the miranda state located in an area called orangel to plant and cultivate

  • 2019 - He decides to make a chicken coop to also dedicate himself to breeding.

  • 2024 - He currently lives happily working in what he really likes working in the fields.
